How We Calculate Income Inequality

Income Inequality is measured in several ways:

0.48

The Gini Coefficient

ranges from 0 to 1.

0 indicates perfect equality - everyone receives an equal share of income.

1 indicates perfect inequality - only one group of people receives all income.

This measure does not take into account family size when calculating income.
