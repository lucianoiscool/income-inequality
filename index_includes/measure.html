How We Measure Income Inequality

There are several ways to measure Income Inequality:

The Gini Coefficient

The most widely used measure, The Gini Coefficient ranges from 0 to 1. 0 indicates perfect equality - everyone receives an equal share of income. 1 indicates perfect inequality - only one group of people receives all the income. This measure does not take into account family size.
